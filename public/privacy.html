Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This Policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, the choices and rights you have, and how to contact us.

Last updated: August 22, 2025

Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the Picuz gaming platform ("Platform", "we", "our", or "us") and covers your use of our website, embedded games, and related services. By accessing or using the Platform you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, please discontinue use.

Key Terms

  • Personal Data: Information that identifies or can reasonably be linked to an individual (e.g., IP address combined with other data, contact email).
  • Usage Data: Metrics about how the Platform is accessed (pages viewed, device type, session duration).
  • Cookies: Small text files stored on your device to remember preferences and enhance performance.

Summary (Quick Overview)

  • We collect limited personal data you provide (e.g., email when contacting us) and technical/usage data automatically.
  • We use analytics and advertising technologies to improve the Platform and keep it free.
  • You can manage cookies and request access, deletion, or correction of your data.
  • We do not sell your personal data, but we may share pseudonymous identifiers with ad/analytics partners.
  • Children under 13 should not submit personal data.

For full details, read the sections below.

Information We Collect

1. Automatically Collected (Usage & Technical)

  • IP address (truncated or masked where feasible)
  • Approximate geolocation (country / region derived from IP)
  • Device and browser identifiers, user-agent string
  • Operating system, screen resolution, language settings
  • Referring / exit pages, navigation paths, time on page
  • Error logs, performance metrics, latency measurements

2. Information You Voluntarily Provide

  • Contact email and message contents when you reach out
  • Feedback, bug reports, feature suggestions
  • Search queries entered into on-site search features

3. Advertising & Analytics Data

Ad networks and analytics partners may collect pseudonymous identifiers (cookie IDs, advertising IDs) to measure impressions, prevent fraud, and personalize ads subject to applicable law and your settings.

4. Third-Party Sources

We may receive aggregated or inferred data (e.g., coarse location or interest segments) from analytics or advertising partners to better understand performance and relevance. We do not merge this with information that directly identifies you unless you contact us.

5. Avoid Sharing Sensitive Data

Please do not submit sensitive categories of personal data (e.g., government IDs, financial account numbers, health data) via open contact channels. We do not intentionally collect such information.

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes collected or to satisfy legal / compliance obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Aggregated or anonymized data may be kept longer for analytics.

No Internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed 100% secure; we strive to use commercially reasonable safeguards.

If you are located in the EEA/UK/Switzerland and transfers occur to a country lacking an adequacy decision, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or equivalent mechanisms adopted by authorities.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located in jurisdictions different from your own. We implement reasonable safeguards consistent with applicable law for cross-border transfers.
